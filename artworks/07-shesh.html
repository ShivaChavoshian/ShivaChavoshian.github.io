Shesh

Size: 27.5 * 39.5 cm

Technique:

  1. Handmade Paper
  2. Gold
  3. Gouache
  4. Natural Colours
  5. Ink

I made this exactly same as the original one which refers to the Il-Khanid dynasty. The materials are all handmade such as natural colours(mineral, herbal and insects) handmade paper and 24 karat gold. With this project which took about 6 months of hard work I learnt a lot about traditional ways of making art and the history of My country. I am so in love with the artworks from this historic time because all of the artworks are full of beautiful and complex geometry.
