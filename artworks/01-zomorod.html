ZOMOROD

Size: 50 * 60 cm

Technique:

  1. Handmade Paper
  2. Gold
  3. Gouache
  4. Watercolour
  5. Ink

Making Zomorrod is one of my favorite art journeys. I worked on the geometry, design and the colours for a long time and I tried to have as much details as possible inside it.

In Tazhib everything is based on symmetry and harmony. I tried a lot to have these.
