Phase 01: The Proof

The Gaming Industry

We started where communities are most demanding. Gamers expect high performance, real-time interaction, and deep gamification. Success in Canada led to EU, Australia, and Africa.

Betway

We successfully launched a social layer on Betway, one of the biggest sportsbooks with millions of users. It featured search, a personalized feed, clips, a social bar, user interaction, and social profiles.

Games Global

As pioneers in online gaming socialization, we deeply studied player behavior. We found that social profiles, group chats, and comments on online gaming were the best fit for these users.

Collaborating with developers and architects, We defined a scalable design system using semantic tokens. This strategic framework allowed the entire product ecosystem to adapt to diverse brand identities.
