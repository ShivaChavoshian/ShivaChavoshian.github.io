Phase 01: The Proof

The Gaming Industry

We started where communities are most demanding. Gamers expect high performance, real-time interaction, and deep gamification.

Betway

Optimized for rich media (gameplay clips, screenshots) and quick interactions like upvotes and threaded replies.

Games Global

A flexible badge and leaderboard system that developers could tweak on the fly to drive retention.

Success in Canada led to EU, Australia, and Africa. But it raised the ultimate design question: How does this scale to any industry?

Phase 02: the expnastion

Scaling to Global Brands

Once proven in Gaming, we pivoted to enterprise retail and sports, adapting the engine for IKEA, Lindex, and MotoGP.

The White-Label Architecture

I refactored the entire UI into a scalable Design System using semantic tokens. This allowed diverse brands to customize colors, radii, and vibe globally via a simple dashboard.

Industry-Agnostic Validation

Led tailored research initiatives for each suite. By validating retail (IKEA) vs. sports (MotoGP), we ensured our core features solved industry-specific problems without breaking the modular layout.

Outcomes

Business Impact

  • Onboarding Velocity: Client launch times reduced from weeks to days via the modular white-label system.
  • Sales Success: Visual demos in exact brand colors directly contributed to signing IKEA and Lindex.
  • Sustainable Growth: Empowered users with a voice and created resale ecosystems through internal brand feeds.
