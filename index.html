About me

Tazhib (Traditional Artworks) is a non-figurative illumination with geometric flourishes, decorations and intricate calligraphy have adorned the margins of holy books and epic poems, as well as being an art form in its own right.

I work on Tazhib artworks using traditional materials such as handmade papers, colours, brushes and gold in collaboration with geometric patterns in Islamic arts.

I am into the history of Tazhib and all different forms of Arabic and Persian calligraphy. My research on this subject helps me create more authentic artworks.

I am so passionate about the History of islamic art, culture and specifically making handmade books.

My current and long term personal project is making a collection, inspired by the best artworks from all historical periods of Islamic art. I want to show how this art has evolved during more than 1400 years.

I started to learn traditional art when I was 13 years old. As an Iraninan I have always been observing the great artworks all around the country. Now I am living in Sweden and I want to represent Tazhib and its culture.

You can find me on my Youtube channel in which I am teaching Tazhib step by step.

And also on my Instagram account I am trying to present and show my artworks and the way that I am making them.
