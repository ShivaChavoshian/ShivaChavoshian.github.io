The Alchemist

Combining Logic & Emotion

I am a Senior Product Designer and Artist based in Stockholm, Sweden. My journey bridges two worlds: the rigorous, user-centric discipline of Product Design and the intricate, meditative precision of Tazhib (traditional illumination art).

I believe that great design, like great art, comes from a deep understanding of structure. Whether I'm architecting a complex SaaS dashboard or painting a geometry of gold leaf, my goal is always the same: Clarity, Beauty, and Meaning.
