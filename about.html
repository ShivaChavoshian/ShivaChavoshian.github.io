Shiva Chvoshian

I am an artist. Tazhib is my favorite kind of painting and I spend lots of time to work and see old Tazhibs and learn from them. My passion is trying to make unique shining artworks full of details. I have started Tazhib when I was 15 years old but it was on and off until 4 years ago. I have started it again and this time I knew it is what I want to do with my life. So I have been continued it near some great teachers and I went to 5 art exhibitions. Tazhib is non-figurative illumination whose geometric flourishes, decorations and intricate calligraphy have traditionally adorned the margins of holy books and epic poems, as well as being an art form in its own right. The harmony and the intense concentration calm me to an extent that I feel great. I believe hardworking with love is key to success.
I am currently working on projects at my homeoffice and I am always intrested in a challenge. Reach out to chavoshian.shiva@gmail.com to connect.
